# Welcome & System Orientation (Start Here)

This document is your soft landing page to orient you when setting up or resuming your **CXO-as-a-Service (Personal C-Suite)** system.

---

## 1. The Core Partnership Model
* **Your Executive Team:** An AI Personal Executive engine running 5 C-Suite lenses:
  * **CEO:** Strategic Vision, 1–3 Year Horizon, CEO Escalation Radar & 3-Strike Deferral Rule.
  * **CFO:** Liquid Runway, Monthly Burn, Taxes, Estate Assets, and Invoicing Lag.
  * **COO / PM:** Active Initiatives, Task Checklists, Household Logistics, and Weekly Stakeholder Status Reports.
  * **CHO:** Holistic Capacity Dashboard (Mind, Body, Spirit telemetry: sleep, movement, spiritual grounding).
  * **CTO:** Tooling, Prompt Workflows, local AI instructions, and Data Architecture.
* **The Tone:** Direct, objective, and analytical. Zero fluff, filler, or sycophancy.
* **The Division of Labor:** You operate as the **Chairman of the Board** (hands-on execution & decision-making), while the AI operates as your **Personal Executive / CXO** (auditing, tracking, probing, and maintaining state).

## 3. Quick Start (3 Steps to Launch)

### Step 1: Initialize Your Vault Files
Copy `01-foundational-requirements.md` into your new vault root.  
Copy `04-living-ledger-template.md` to your vault root and rename it to `living-ledger.md`.  
Create your three archive subdirectories: `archives/user-surveys/`, `archives/feedback-logs/`, and `archives/misc/`.

### Step 2: Feed System Instructions to Your AI
Upload [01-foundational-requirements.md](file:///01-foundational-requirements.md) to your AI model (or set it as your System Prompt) and issue the priming prompt:
> *"You are my Personal Executive functioning as my CXO-as-a-Service. Please read and adopt the rules of engagement in 01-foundational-requirements.md. I want to begin onboarding."*

### Step 3: Complete Stage 1 Intake
Open [03-onboarding-survey-stage-1.md](file:///03-onboarding-survey-stage-1.md), fill in your responses, and submit it to your AI to generate your customized C-Suite matrix and initialize your `living-ledger.md`.
