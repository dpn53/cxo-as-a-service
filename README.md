# CXO-as-a-Service (Personal C-Suite): Onboarding & Setup Guide

Welcome to the **CXO-as-a-Service (Personal C-Suite)** framework. This system is designed to turn any advanced AI chat assistant (such as Antigravity or a local LLM setup) into your personal executive team-acting as your CEO, CFO, COO, and Chief Health Officer.

By adopting specialized executive "lenses" during check-ins, this system audits your professional projects, financial runway, daily operations, and holistic capacity (sleep, exercise, spiritual grounding) in a structured, unified conversation.

---

## 1. Directory Structure

To spin up your own instance of the Personal C-Suite, create a dedicated folder (e.g., in Obsidian, Notion, or your local filesystem) with the following structure:

```text
/
├── foundational_requirements.md   <-- Copy from this folder (Rules of Engagement)
├── document_inventory.md          <-- Copy from this folder (Document Directory & Index)
├── living_ledger.md               <-- Copy living_ledger_template.md & rename
├── user_survey_archive/           <-- Folder for dated diagnostic surveys
├── feedback_archive/              <-- Folder for raw voice/text updates
└── misc_archive/                  <-- Folder for experimental prompts & reference files
```

---

## 2. File Roles & Descriptions

1. **`document_inventory.md` (Document Index & Directory):**  
   The central reference map detailing the exact purpose, lifecycle, operational workflow, and quick reference table for all files in the system.
2. **`foundational_requirements.md` (System Instructions):**  
   Defines the AI's persona, communication constraints (direct, no fluff, no sycophancy), decision-support rules, and the **3-Strike Deferral Rule** for breaking cognitive ruts. Feed this file to your AI assistant as its master instructions.
3. **`living_ledger.md` (Single Source of Truth):**  
   Tracks project checklists, cash runway metrics, active stakeholder CRMs, and your holistic well-being dashboard. Kept permanently in the root folder.
4. **`user_survey_archive/` (Diagnostic Archive):**  
   Contains point-in-time diagnostic surveys generated by the AI to probe your progress.
5. **`feedback_archive/` (Speech-to-Text Raw Log):**  
   Stores raw voice/text dictation updates. The AI automatically processes these, updates your ledger, and moves them here to keep the root directory clean.

---

## 3. How to Initialize the System

### Step 1: Set Up Files
1. Copy `foundational_requirements.md` from this directory to your new vault root.
2. Copy `living_ledger_template.md` to your vault root and rename it to `living_ledger.md`.
3. Create the empty directories: `user_survey_archive/`, `feedback_archive/`, and `misc_archive/`.

### Step 2: Feed Instructions to Your AI Assistant
* Upload `foundational_requirements.md` to your AI workspace.
* Enter the following system priming prompt:
  > *"You are my Personal Executive functioning as my CXO-as-a-Service. Please read and adopt the rules of engagement in foundational_requirements.md. I want to begin onboarding."*

### Step 3: Run the Multi-Stage Onboarding Survey
To customize the C-Suite roles to your specific life context (e.g., a student's CFO/COO roles look very different from a business contractor's), follow this onboarding workflow:

1. **Discovery (Stage 1):** Open [onboarding_survey_stage1.md](file:///onboarding_survey_stage1.md) (in this folder), fill out your responses, and paste it into the AI.
2. **Role Mapping (Stage 2):** The AI will process your Stage 1 inputs and output a **Customized C-Suite Role Mapping Matrix** showing exactly what your CEO, CFO, COO, and CHO will manage.
3. **Ledger Initialization (Stage 3):** The AI will populate your initial `living_ledger.md` based on your Stage 2 choices. Copy that generated structure into your blank `living_ledger.md` to begin daily operations.
