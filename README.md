Welcome to the **CXO-as-a-Service (Personal C-Suite)** framework. This system transforms any advanced AI assistant (such as Antigravity or a local LLM setup) into your personal executive team—acting as your CEO, CFO, COO, CHO, and CTO.

By adopting specialized executive "lenses" during check-ins, this system audits your professional projects, financial runway, daily operations, and holistic capacity (sleep, exercise, spiritual grounding) in a structured, unified conversation.

---

## 1. Vault Directory Structure & Numbered Sequence

To spin up your instance of the Personal C-Suite in Obsidian, Notion, or your local workspace, create a folder with the following numbered file structure:

```text
/ (Personal C-Suite Vault)
├── README.md                           <-- Vault setup & repository overview
├── 00-start-here.md                    <-- Soft landing orientation guide
├── 01-foundational-requirements.md     <-- Rules of Engagement & System Prompt
├── 02-document-inventory.md            <-- Document Directory & Architectural Index
├── 03-onboarding-survey-stage-1.md     <-- Discovery intake survey
├── 04-living-ledger-template.md        <-- Template for living-ledger.md
├── 05-user-survey-template.md          <-- Diagnostic check-in survey template
└── archives/
    ├── user-surveys/                   <-- Dated diagnostic survey repository
    ├── feedback-logs/                  <-- Processed speech-to-text dictations
    └── misc/                           <-- Reference materials & prompt sandbox
```

---

## 2. File Roles & Architectural Overview

1. **`00-start-here.md` (Executive Orientation):**  
   Your landing document introducing the Chairman vs. Personal Executive partnership model and step-by-step setup instructions.
2. **`01-foundational-requirements.md` (System Instructions):**  
   Defines the AI's persona, communication constraints (direct, no fluff, no sycophancy), decision-support rules, and the **3-Strike Deferral Rule** for breaking cognitive ruts. Feed this file to your AI assistant as its master instructions.
3. **`02-document-inventory.md` (Document Directory Index):**  
   The central reference map detailing the purpose, lifecycle, operational workflow, and quick reference table for all files in the system.
4. **`03-onboarding-survey-stage-1.md` (Discovery Intake):**  
   Initial open-ended intake survey used during onboarding to generate your customized C-Suite Role Mapping Matrix.
5. **`04-living-ledger-template.md` (Single Source of Truth Template):**  
   Template copied to `living-ledger.md` to track project checklists, cash runway metrics, active stakeholder CRMs, and your holistic well-being dashboard.
6. **`05-user-survey-template.md` (Diagnostic Survey Blueprint):**  
   Template used by the AI to generate point-in-time diagnostic surveys (`user-survey-YYYYMMDD.md`).

## 3. How to Initialize the System

### Step 1: Set Up Vault Files
1. Copy `01-foundational-requirements.md` to your vault root.
2. Copy `04-living-ledger-template.md` to your vault root and rename it to `living-ledger.md`.
3. Create your three archive directories: `archives/user-surveys/`, `archives/feedback-logs/`, and `archives/misc/`.

### Step 2: Feed Instructions to Your AI Assistant
* Upload `01-foundational-requirements.md` to your AI workspace.
* Enter the following system priming prompt:
  > *"You are my Personal Executive functioning as my CXO-as-a-Service. Please read and adopt the rules of engagement in 01-foundational-requirements.md. I want to begin onboarding."*

### Step 3: Run the Multi-Stage Onboarding Survey
Follow this 3-stage onboarding workflow:

1. **Discovery (Stage 1):** Open [03-onboarding-survey-stage-1.md](file:///03-onboarding-survey-stage-1.md), fill out your responses, and paste it to the AI.
2. **Role Mapping (Stage 2):** The AI will process your Stage 1 inputs and output a **Customized C-Suite Role Mapping Matrix** showing exactly what your CEO, CFO, COO, CHO, and CTO will manage.
3. **Ledger Initialization (Stage 3):** The AI will populate your initial `living-ledger.md` based on your Stage 2 choices. Copy that generated structure into your blank `living-ledger.md` to begin daily operations.
